About Me


Back when I was in college and I was deciding on my major, I was at a crossroads. I wanted to study both Accounting and Computer Science. I was interested in both fields but I knew that I could choose only one for my career path. I ended up choosing the former and I have been an accountant for over the past six years. But Computer Science always lingered in my mind and I never stopped wondering whether or not I made the right decision. Over the past couple of years I have become less interested in Accounting and more interested in Web Development. So I decided to enroll in The George Washington University's Coding Bootcamp as I was ready for a career change.

Now that I have been immersed in the Web Development field for the last several months, I love it. It seems like what I am able to create with the skills that I have acquired are limitless. I want to learn as much as possible, which will be an ongoing process for me. Those acquired skills so far include HTML, CSS, JavaScript, jQuery, Node.js, Express.js, and MySQL just to name a few. I always want to be learning and growing as a Web Developer and increase my skillset in order to perfect my craft.
